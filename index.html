our Selection

We use only the finest leaves from Cuba, Peru and Nicaragua.
All our leaves are aged for a minimum of 2 years
and are derived from 100% Cuban seed.

our Heritage

To continue the Casdagli tradition Emanuel Casdagli’s
great grandson, Jeremy Nicholas Casdagli,
launched the Bespoke cigar range in 2009.
