Robusto

Ring: 50mm
Length: 124mm
Weight: 12.67g

The most popular vitola smoked today. Our robusto has extra aged Seco leaf and delivers a rich , sweet honey taste. This is a medium to mild cigar and is a perfect smoke throughout the day.

This cigar is blended especially for Bespoke cigars by Hendrik Kelner Jr of the famous Kelner family of Master blenders at the KBF factory.
